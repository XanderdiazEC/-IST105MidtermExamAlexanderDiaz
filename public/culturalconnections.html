Alexander's Cultural Experience

Shock ExperiencesTwo

  1. A lot of people from different countries.
    • It is fascinating to see many different physical features in person and experience various cultures. I love having different conversations with each one because everyone has unique stories about their culture and food, which provides an incredible perspective on the world.
  2. The security.
    • I'm from Ecuador, a country where safety can be a concern. Here in Canada, I can go out with my phone without the fear of being stolen. I love this feeling of security.

Two Things I Miss from Ecuador

  1. The food
    • I really miss the traditional food, especially "encebollado" (a traditional fish soup) that I enjoyed every weekend. It’s my favorite dish from Ecuador, and I miss eating things like "bolón."
  2. The locations
    • I miss visiting the beautiful beaches in Ecuador and places like "Quilotoa," a stunning lake located in a volcano. I also miss the Amazon rainforest and the unique culture of the people living there.

I Love Vancouver

  • I love this beautiful city with countless activities to do and many parks to explore.

My Personal Goal
